For restaurants, grocery stores, c-stores, and food service businesses, reliable refrigeration is
the backbone of the whole operation. A failure in your walk-in cooler or freezer doesn't just mean
a repair bill — it can mean thousands of dollars in spoiled inventory and lost revenue while you're
down. **NewGen HVAC** specializes in protecting that investment, and it's the work most residential
HVAC shops can't help you with.

## Comprehensive refrigeration service

We install, maintain, and repair the full range of commercial refrigeration equipment:

- **Walk-in coolers and freezers** — kept within the strict temperature ranges food safety requires.
- **Reach-in units** — the workhorses of any kitchen line.
- **Ice machines** — including Hoshizaki and Manitowoc, installed, cleaned, and repaired.
- **Evaporators, condensers, and compressors** — diagnosed and fixed by technicians who know
  refrigeration as its own discipline, not a sideline.

## Why preventive care wins

Here's the math that matters: a planned maintenance visit costs a fraction of a single emergency
breakdown plus the inventory you lose with it. Regular checks for refrigerant leaks, dirty coils,
and worn mechanical parts catch the problems that cause those failures — usually weeks before they
would have shut you down. For a busy kitchen, that predictability is the difference between a
scheduled tune-up and a Friday-night disaster.
