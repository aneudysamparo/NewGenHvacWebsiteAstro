Your home is your sanctuary, and a comfortable, consistent temperature is a big part of what makes
it one. At **NewGen HVAC**, we offer residential HVAC solutions built for New England homes and
New England weather — and we explain your options clearly, in English or Spanish.

## Efficient heating and cooling

Today's homeowners have better options than the old central system that either roasts or freezes
you:

- **Mini-split systems** give you room-by-room control, so you stop paying to heat and cool space
  no one is using. They're quiet, efficient, and great for additions, finished basements, and older
  homes without ductwork.
- **Cold-climate heat pumps** handle our winters and do the job of both a heater and an AC in one
  efficient system. In Massachusetts, qualifying installs can also unlock significant rebates —
  worth asking about before you replace anything.
- **Traditional furnace service and prep** keeps your existing system safe and ready before the
  first cold snap.

## Don't forget the air you breathe

Comfort isn't only about temperature. Indoor air quality solutions — better filtration, humidity
control, and air cleaners — help reduce allergens and pollutants, which matters most in the months
when the house is sealed up tight. If anyone in your home deals with allergies or asthma, this is
an easy upgrade with a real payoff.
