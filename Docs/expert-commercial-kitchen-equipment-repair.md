A commercial kitchen is a high-pressure environment where every piece of equipment has a job to
do. When a fryer, oven, or griddle goes down mid-service, it doesn't just slow you down — it knocks
items off your menu and eats into your revenue. **NewGen HVAC** provides fast, reliable repair to
keep your kitchen firing on all cylinders.

## Expertise you can trust

Our technicians service a wide range of commercial cooking equipment: fryers, ovens, griddles,
flat-tops, and more — along with the components that make them work, like pilot assemblies,
burners, ignition systems, and gas valves. We understand that kitchen downtime is expensive, so we
prioritize these calls and work to get you back up as quickly as possible.

## Safety and calibration

Repairs are only half the job. We also calibrate thermostats and safety controls so your equipment
runs safely and consistently. Proper calibration extends the life of your appliances and keeps your
cooking results predictable plate after plate. And when there's a gas issue, safety comes first —
we detect and repair gas leaks in your equipment and piping before anything else.

## A quick word on prevention

The same equipment that fails at the worst possible moment usually gave warning signs first — a
burner that's slow to light, a fryer that won't hold temperature, an oven that's drifting off its
setpoint. Calling early, before a small issue becomes a breakdown, is almost always cheaper than an
emergency repair during a rush.
