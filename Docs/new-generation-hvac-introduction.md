Welcome to **NewGen HVAC**, your local partner for heating, cooling, and commercial refrigeration
across Massachusetts and Southern New Hampshire. Founded in 2014 by **Jean Carlos Alcántara**,
we've spent more than a decade building a reputation for honest work, fast response, and service
that actually fits our community — in English and Spanish.

## Who we are

We're not a franchise or a call center. We're an owner-run shop based in Methuen, and when you
call, you reach the people who do the work. Whether it's a family home's heat pump or a
restaurant's walk-in cooler, we bring real technical know-how and a commitment to doing the job
right the first time.

## What we do

- **Commercial & residential HVAC** — From rooftop units and Mitsubishi Hyper-Heat systems to
  home mini-splits and furnace diagnostics, we keep your space at the right temperature year-round.
- **Commercial refrigeration** — Walk-in coolers, freezers, reach-ins, and ice machines, kept
  running so your inventory stays safe. This is our specialty, and it's work most residential HVAC
  shops simply can't do.
- **Commercial kitchen equipment** — Fryers, ovens, griddles, and the burners, valves, and safety
  controls behind them, back up and running fast.
- **The Comfort Plan** — A residential maintenance membership that keeps your home comfortable all
  year and gets ahead of expensive breakdowns.

## What makes us different

Two things set us apart in this market. First, we're **fully bilingual** — we serve our
Spanish-speaking neighbors the way they deserve, with clear answers and no detail lost in
translation. Second, we do **real commercial refrigeration**, which means restaurants, markets,
and food businesses have one trusted shop for both their HVAC and the cooling equipment they can't
afford to lose.
