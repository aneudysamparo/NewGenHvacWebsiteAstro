# Per-town page template

> The #1 SEO mistake on the old site was 35 near-identical town pages (including towns you don't
> serve). Quality beats quantity: each page needs a genuine local hook, or Google treats them as
> thin/duplicate content. Use this structure and the per-town hooks below. Don't just swap the name.

**Slug:** /service-areas/[town-state]   (e.g. /service-areas/methuen-ma)
**Title:** HVAC & Refrigeration in [Town], [ST] | NewGen HVAC
**Meta:** Bilingual HVAC, commercial refrigeration & kitchen equipment service in [Town], [ST]. Family-run, fast response, 24/7 emergency. Call (978) 876-8558.

**Sections:**
1. **H1:** HVAC & Commercial Refrigeration in [Town], [ST]
2. **Local intro** — 2–3 sentences using the town's real hook (below): who we serve there, distance/response from Methuen, any local angle (restaurants, older housing stock, etc.).
3. **Services we provide in [Town]** — the 4 services, each linking to its service page.
4. **Why [Town] chooses NewGen** — bilingual, refrigeration specialty, local & fast, since 2014.
5. **Nearby areas** — link 3–4 adjacent town pages (internal linking).
6. **CTA** — Free estimate + tap-to-call.
7. **LocalBusiness JSON-LD** with the town in `areaServed`.
