# Testimonials

**Do not ship the old site's testimonials.** The previous site used three placeholder names
(Mike Ross, Carlos Mendez, Sarah Jenkins) with one repeated stock avatar. Fake or generic
testimonials hurt trust and can violate review-platform and FTC advertising rules.

## What to do instead

Populate the Directus **`testimonials`** collection from **real Google reviews**:
- Fields: `name`, `role` (e.g. "Restaurant Owner, Lawrence"), `quote`, `rating`, `source: Google`, `date`, `featured`.
- Pull your strongest 4–6 reviews to feature on the homepage and service pages.
- Where a reviewer is a restaurant/commercial client, label it — it reinforces the refrigeration
  and kitchen-equipment positioning.

## Build a steady review flow (this is the real lever)

Your Review Request Engine targets moving the Google rating from ~4.2 toward 4.7+. Wire the new
site and your stack to feed it:
- Add a "Leave us a Google review" link in the footer and on post-service follow-ups.
- Trigger a review request via GoHighLevel/WhatsApp after each completed Jobber job.
- Feature new 5-star reviews on the site automatically as they come in.

A consistent stream of recent, real reviews does more for local ranking and conversion than any
amount of polished copy — and it's nearly free.
