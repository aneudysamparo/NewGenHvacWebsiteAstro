## Comfort Plan pricing (PUBLIC — this is a product page, prices are shown)
> Frozen structure from "Comfort Plan Pricing Structure v2.0." No discounting, no rounding
> ($348 is never $349). Residential only.
- **Comfort Plan:** $249/yr or $24.99/mo — 1 seasonal tune-up per system/year
- **Comfort Plan Plus:** $348/yr or $34.98/mo — 2 seasonal tune-ups per system/year
- **Additional System Add-On:** +$99/yr or +$9.99/mo per *independent* system
- **System-counting rule:** a heat pump = ONE system (it heats and cools on the same equipment).
  An extra independent unit (2nd furnace, separate-outdoor-unit mini-split, 2nd condenser on its
  own air handler) = +1 add-on. Accessories (humidifier, UV, air cleaner) are never a system.
- Members get **priority scheduling** and **member pricing on repairs.**
