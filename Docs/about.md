# Our story
NewGen HVAC (New Generation HVAC LLC) was founded in 2014 by Jean Carlos Alcántara with a simple
idea: give homeowners and business owners a technician who actually knows the equipment, shows up
when promised, and explains things in plain language — in English or Spanish. More than a decade
later, that's still how we work.

We're not a franchise or a call center. We're a local shop based in Methuen, and when you call,
you're dealing with the people who do the work.

# What makes us different
- **Bilingual service.** We serve our heavily Spanish-speaking community the way it deserves — full
  service in English and Español, with no detail lost in translation.
- **Commercial refrigeration expertise.** Walk-in coolers, freezers, and ice machines are their own
  discipline. Most residential HVAC companies don't touch them. We specialize in them, which means
  restaurants, markets, and food businesses have one trusted shop for the systems they can't afford to lose.
- **Honest, practical work.** We diagnose the real problem, quote it fairly, and stand behind the fix.

# Who we serve
Homeowners and businesses across the Merrimack Valley — Methuen, Lawrence, Haverhill, Andover,
North Andover, Lowell, and surrounding towns — plus Salem, Windham, Derry, and other Southern New
Hampshire communities. From a family home's heat pump to a restaurant's walk-in and rooftop units,
we cover it.

# What we handle
- Commercial & residential HVAC — rooftop units, mini-splits, heat pumps, furnaces, AC
- Commercial refrigeration — walk-ins, reach-ins, ice machines, leak detection, compressors
- Commercial kitchen equipment — fryers, ovens, griddles, gas valves, safety controls
- Residential maintenance memberships — the Comfort Plan

# How we work
1. **Consultation** — We assess your heating, cooling, or refrigeration needs and give you a straight answer.
2. **Service or install** — Done right the first time, with quality parts and clean workmanship.
3. **Maintenance** — Seasonal tune-ups that catch small problems before they become expensive ones.
4. **Emergency support** — 24/7 response for the equipment that can't wait.
